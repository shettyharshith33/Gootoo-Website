About Gootoo

Gootoo is a modern doctor appointment booking platform that connects patients with doctors seamlessly. With Gootoo, patients can view doctor availability, choose convenient slots, and receive instant booking confirmation. Doctors can manage their schedules efficiently and patients enjoy a smooth healthcare experience.

Features

Easy Booking

Book doctor appointments in just a few taps.

Slot Management

Doctors can set daily availability and time slots.

Instant Confirmation

Get instant token confirmation with date & time.

Secure Payments

Pay securely using Razorpay integration.
